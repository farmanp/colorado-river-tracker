## Notes

### Response Window
- Initial outreach: 7-10 business days for response
- Follow-up: If no response after 10 days, send one reminder
- After 14 days: Note "Did not respond to requests for comment"

### Journalistic Standards
- "Declined to comment" is publishable
- "No response" after 14 days is publishable
- Record ALL responses verbatim
- Flag any factual disputes immediately for verification

### Publishing Decision
Once all 8 agencies have either:
1. Responded, or
2. Exceeded 14-day window

Then proceed to final review and publication.
